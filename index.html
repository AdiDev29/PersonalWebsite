About

I'm an IB student with a focus on mathematics and computer science. Originally from Cluj-Napoca, Romania, now based in Toronto.

I've been doing competitive programming for a while now and genuinely enjoy the problem solving side of it. I take on leadership roles when I feel like I can actually contribute something. DECA, robotics, music council, among others. Building things with people is just more interesting than going at it alone.
